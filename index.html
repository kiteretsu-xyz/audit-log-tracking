// core features

Why on-chain
audit logging?

🔒
Tamper-proof records
Once written to Nero Chain, no entry can be altered. The blockchain provides cryptographic guarantees that your audit trail is immutable and trustworthy.
⚡
Real-time flagging
Flag suspicious entries instantly with a reason string. The contract enforces that an entry can only be flagged once, preventing duplicate alerts.
🔍
Queryable state
Read entry counts, flagged totals, and individual records via read-only simulation — no gas cost for queries. Perfect for dashboards and monitoring.
🛡️
Auth-gated writes
Every write operation requires the actor to sign the transaction. Only the actor or auditor who calls a function can be its author — spoofing is impossible.
📊
Severity levels 0–5
Classify events from informational (0–1) through warning (2) to critical (3–5). The contract validates range on-chain, so bad data never reaches storage.
🌐
Nero Chain native
Built in Solidity on Nero Chain — EVM compatible smart contract platform. 5-second finality, low fees, and full testnet support out of the box.
